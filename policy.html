Privacy Policy

MagnetMind Media (“we”, “our”) collects only the data required to authenticate with TikTok and to publish content you explicitly approve. We do not sell personal data.

Information We Collect

  • TikTok account basic info returned by OAuth (e.g., user ID, access token).
  • Content you provide to post (captions, media).

How We Use Information

  • To let you sign in with TikTok and post content via Direct Post.
  • To comply with platform policies and legal requirements.

Data Sharing

No sale of data. We share only with service providers strictly necessary to operate the app.

Data Retention & Deletion

Access tokens are stored securely and deleted upon disconnection. You can request deletion at: magnetmind.oficial@gmail.com.
